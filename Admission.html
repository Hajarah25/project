ADMISSION PROCEDURES

At Darool Uloom School,the following steps provide advise on our admission procedure


1. The New Pupil enquiry form should be filled in and submit to the School Admin Staff.


2. The Prospective Parent/Guardian is given a tour of the school by the Admin Officer.


3. An appointment is scheduled for the applicant to sit for an assessment test.
